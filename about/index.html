About me

Hi, I'm Hoang

I love applying data science practices to solve fisheries problems. At present, I am on my PhD journey with Fisheries Quantitative Lab at Marine Institute, Memorial University of Newfoundland, enjoy the discovery of statistical modelling with R and Python for quantitative analysis and visualization. I believe in the importance of domain knowledge in data science work, and good data work must rely on a thorough understanding of the field.

This site is where I document my data science journey and share some of my work in fisheries management, sustainable marine farming, and environmental science.
